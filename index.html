OPD

OPD OPD offers a very wide variety of services such as clinical examination, investigations, diagnosis, minor surgical procedure, interventional procedures, counselling and rehabilitation services. Efficient and effective functioning of OPD promotes relief to patients, reduces burden on the indoor services and it is the first point of interaction between the patient and the medical staff. It is situated in a separate building to prevent the mixing up with the indoor patients.

Laboratory

The laboratories are facilitated with technologically advanced equipments. Various services like vital organ screening, diabetes screening, vitamin deficiency testing etc are provided.

RADIOLOGY AND X-RAY FACILITIES

Radiology department is the specialized department that uses medical imaging to diagnose and treat diseases within the body. A variety of imaging techniques such as X- ray radiography, ultrasound and magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) are used to diagnose or treat diseases. Ambulance

We have Ambulance vehicles for rent such as on demand, Patient pick up & drop, etc. We strongly believe that in emergency care, seconds can save lives. We believe that no patient has to wait for specialised care which is why we work based on the triage process.

ECG SERVICES

This area of the Cardio-Respiratory department is responsible for performing non-invasive testing on the electrical system of your heart through a variety of tests. It is staffed by highly skilled and dedicated professionals. We provide a comprehensive range of non-Invasive Cardiology, Respiratory and Vascular investigations to in-patients, out-patients and General Practitioners.

PHARMACY

We provide an appropriate supply of quality medications for all patients in an effective and efficient manner. The department provides pharmaceutical care to all patients in accordance with local laws and regulations. Pharmacy services are continually upgraded with emphasis on the rational, safe, and economic use of medications.

OPERATION THEATRE

The operation theatre complex is satisfactorily equipped to handle major and minor surgeries. We cover a comprehensive range of surgical specialities. Our OT department provides outstanding surgical facilities 24x7. This support team works closely with the surgeons and anaesthetists to assure the best possible service to the patients.
