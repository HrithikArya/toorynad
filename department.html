Cardiac sciences

Heart is the most vital organ of our body so taking care of it should be of utmost importance. We offer a broad range of cardiac services including interventional cardiology and electrophysiology by adopting customized patient care where expert cardiologists diagnose and treat the various cardiovascular diseases.

Pediatrics

Newborns and children often feel helpless when they are unable to express their illness. Pediatricians are specially trained to handle all types of cases - emergency and routine. We offer a wide range of services to all age groups, from a newborn baby till 18 years of age like pediatric ICU, Neonatal ICU, Immunisation and a dedicated pediatric emergency room.

Anesthesiology

Anesthesia is the main component of any surgical process as it dictates the smooth and pain-free recovery of surgical patients. The dose and the site of administering anesthesia should be planned well keeping in mind the requirement of the surgery and the condition of the patient. Experienced anesthesiologists are available 24x7.

Neurological sciences

Our aim is to improve the lives of those patients who have been diagnosed with neurological conditions. Our emergency services include treatment that deals with trauma, stroke, seizures and infections. We also offer services related to tumors, spinal disorders, epilepsy etc.

Dermatology

Disorders of skin, hair and nails may be due to allergies, infections and endocrine disorders. Our main focus is on the diagnosis, treatment and prevention of disorders of the skin, hair and nails. The treatment is supported by technologically advanced equipments.

Orthopedics

Injuries-, wear-and-tear and damage to the musculoskeletal system can restrict our physical capabilities to a great extent. Our main aim is to diagnose and treat the musculoskeletal disorders. This includes abnormalities in the bones, muscles, ligaments and other elements that aid in movement.,
